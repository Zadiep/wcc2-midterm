Concept

Tattoo has gradually become a trend in young people. But choosing styles of tattoo always makes youngsters headache, especially for those who want to use special data as tattoo. Our team would like to design a website that can generate tattoos. Users can enter data that they feel are meaningful to them on the web page to generate random tattoos, which can be put on body parts to show the results.
